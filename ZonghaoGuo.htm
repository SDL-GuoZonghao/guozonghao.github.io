Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate of PRISDL in the School of Electronic, Electrical and Communication Engineering, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences , advised by Prof. Qixiang Ye. I got a B.E. degree in Wuhan University, Wuhan in June 2019.

My research interests include computer vision and deep learning, specifically for object detection and instance segmentation.
